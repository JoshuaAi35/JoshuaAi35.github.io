Going through my work you will notice that I challenge  myself with multiple media. From the traditional painting and drawing to a more modern digital media. Yet my specialties have always been with charcoal and ink. There is something about these two mediums that I have always gravitated towards. One of those reasons I feel is because of the impacting contrast between the lights and darks.

All paintings, drawings, and illustrations are for sell. Contact me if you see something you like and or if you would like to commission me for a piece.
