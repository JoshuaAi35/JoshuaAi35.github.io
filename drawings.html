My charcoal and ink drawings I feel are my strongest pieces for multiple reasons. For starters I have always loved the two and have been able to get more out of them. In the sense of detail, effects, textures, emotion, and in rendering different forms. I also work the fastest and most efficiently with the mediums; thus allowing me to create more with them.
